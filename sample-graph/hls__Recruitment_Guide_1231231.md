- Wherever you find a job, whether it’s through our process or elsewhere, please report it to us so that we can report it to the Department for Education as an outcome. We need to provide evidence that everyone has had an interview (an email or calendar invite will do), and eventually been successful into a job (a job offer or a contract). This helps prove to them that our free bootcamp programme is the way forward, and helps us make the case for more funding so we can give more opportunities to more people!
