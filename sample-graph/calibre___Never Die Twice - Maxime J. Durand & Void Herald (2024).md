- [[Synopsis]]
	- <p><strong>Walter Tye will destroy death, even if he has to kill everyone first.</strong>  </p>
	  <p>The last member of an ancient order of necromancers, Walter Tye has a goal: to achieve immortality for all mortals beings and throw off the yoke of the gods deciding the fate of mortals' afterlife. Forced to flee from paladins and witch-hunters, he must hide his identity under the cover of a humble shopkeeper, selling potions to fund his research.  </p>
	  <p>His quiet life of deceit and research is finally shaken with the discovery of an ancient city below ground; one that may hold the gods' most forbidden secret. But beware of paladin blues...</p>
